### rg / ripgrep
- High-performance text search tool, suitable for quickly locating symbols, config keys, and error messages in large repositories.
- Finding functions, classes, commands, or config keys
- Searching for error text in logs
- Determining scope of impact before modifying code

### ffmpeg
- General-purpose audio/video processing tool for transcoding, frame extraction, trimming, concatenation, audio extraction, and similar tasks.
- Processing screen recordings, demo videos, or voice clips
- Converting video to formats better suited for analysis or upload
- Preprocessing audio for speech recognition or summarization pipelines

### yt-dlp
- Downloads publicly accessible online video/audio and can extract subtitles or audio tracks.
- Archiving public video or audio
- Passing downloads to `ffmpeg` for further processing
- Getting subtitles, audio tracks, or metadata for analysis
